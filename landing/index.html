How it works

Three steps to your reel

No editing skills required. Our AI handles the heavy lifting so you can focus on what matters.

1

Upload or describe

2

AI generates your reel

Features

Everything you need to create

Powerful tools that make video creation accessible to everyone.

PDF & text input

Upload any PDF document or paste text directly. Our parser extracts key information and structures it for video narration.

AI-powered scripts

Advanced language models distill your content into concise, engaging scripts perfectly timed for short-form video.

Auto video generation

AI selects relevant visuals, applies transitions, and generates a polished reel ready for TikTok, Reels, or Shorts.

Quick topic prompts

No PDF? No problem. Just type a topic like "Explain quantum computing" and get a video reel generated from scratch.
