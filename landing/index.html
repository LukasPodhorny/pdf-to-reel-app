Built in

More than just narrated slides

A few things you'd expect to need another app for — already inside PDF to Reel.

Batch generation

Turn one prompt or document into a whole playlist — AI splits your topic into chapters and renders multiple reels in a single run.

Pick your favorite avatar

Choose from a library of animated characters — find a voice and vibe that matches your audience, or let AI pick one that fits the topic.

Manim math animations

For math, physics, and CS, the engine animates equations, graphs, and proofs using Manim — the same renderer behind 3Blue1Brown.

Auto-quiz at the end

Optionally close each reel with an AI-generated quiz question — great for revision and for keeping viewers watching all the way through.
